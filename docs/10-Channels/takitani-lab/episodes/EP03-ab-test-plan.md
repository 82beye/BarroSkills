# EP03 A/B Test - 무전술 대감독 vs 경기장 판타지 드리블

## Marketing Decision

Run A/B around the first emotional promise:

- **A안: 전술 풍자형** - "전술판이 비어 있었다"로 시작.
- **B안: 경기장 판타지 드리블형** - "참다못한 선비가 직접 골 넣는다"로 시작.

My recommendation: **produce B first**.

Reason:

- Reels는 첫 1초에 사건이 보여야 한다.
- "감독 무전술"은 공감은 강하지만 설명형으로 흐를 수 있다.
- "관중석에서 응원하다가 상상 속 경기장으로 들어가 골"은 한 컷만 봐도 이해된다.
- 불법 난입을 직접 미화하지 않고도, 난입처럼 보이는 강한 판타지 훅을 만들 수 있다.

## Safety / Platform Rule

Do not depict a realistic illegal pitch invasion as a cool real-world action.

Use one of these framing devices:

- The stadium transforms into a glowing tactical-board dream pitch.
- The scholar is sucked into the manager's blank tactic board.
- The moment is a "what fans imagine" fantasy replay.
- The crowd and pitch are surreal, stylized, and impossible.

Do not show:

- Security guards being dodged or defeated.
- How to enter the field.
- Real stadium gates, fences, or operational details.
- Real team/player/coach likenesses or official marks.

## A안 - 전술 풍자형

### Hook

```text
전술판은 비어 있었고, 말은 웅장했다.
```

### Structure

1. Blank tactic board.
2. Endless "경우의 수" scroll.
3. Sundial substitution.
4. Motivational sideline.
5. VAR royal edict.
6. Blank scroll press conference.

### Strength

- Sharper criticism.
- Safer politically and visually.
- Works well with captions and comments.

### Weakness

- It can feel like a commentary video if visuals are too static.
- First second is less explosive than direct football action.

### Use If

- We want stronger satire.
- We want lower platform risk.
- We expect Korean football discourse comments.

## B안 - 경기장 판타지 드리블형

### Hook

```text
응원하다 참다못한 조선 선비가 직접 골을 넣었다.
```

### Core Promise

The scholar sees the blank tactic board, gets frustrated in the stands, and is pulled into a surreal tactical-board pitch. He dribbles through abstract defenders and scores the goal that the strategy never produced.

### Structure

1. **Cold open:** scholar in red crowd, eyes locked on the pitch, drumbeat rising.
2. **Fantasy trigger:** blank tactic board flashes; the stadium becomes a glowing tactical-board pitch.
3. **Dribble:** scholar runs in full white robes and black gat, ball at feet, sleeves flying.
4. **Goal:** he scores into a generic glowing goal; crowd explodes.
5. **Cutback:** manager still points at a blank board, as if that was the plan.
6. **Press room:** blank scroll is presented as "the next plan."

### Strength

- Strongest thumbnail and first-second hook.
- More physical comedy and replay value.
- Easier for non-Korean viewers to understand.
- Comments likely: "선비가 제일 잘하네", "감독보다 낫다", "국대 소환해라".

### Weakness

- Must clearly be fantasy.
- If rendered too realistically, it may look like field invasion glorification.

### Use If

- We want reach and shares.
- We want more visual action than dialogue satire.
- We can render it as surreal, dreamlike, and obviously impossible.

## A/B Measurement

Compare after 60 minutes and 24 hours:

| Metric | Winner Signal |
|---|---|
| 3-second hold | Hook strength |
| Average watch time | Story clarity |
| Replays | Visual gag strength |
| Shares/sends | Meme potential |
| Comments | Debate potential |
| Saves | Lower priority for this comedy format |

Decision rule:

- If B has higher 3-second hold and shares, keep action-first episodes.
- If A has higher comments and completion, make the next episode more satirical.
- If both underperform, open with the goal first, then explain the blank tactic board after.
