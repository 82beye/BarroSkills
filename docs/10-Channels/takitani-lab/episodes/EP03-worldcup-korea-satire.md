# EP03 - 무전술 대감독과 경우의 수

## Concept

조선 선비가 한국 축구 작전회의에 소환된다. 그는 "경우의 수"를 계산하러 왔지만, 회의실에는 전술 대신 빈 전술판, 늦은 교체 카드, 정신력 멘트만 남아 있다.

이 에피소드는 실존 감독을 직접 복제하거나 실명 조롱하지 않는다. 현재 한국 축구 대표팀을 둘러싼 전술 비판, 태도 비판, 책임론을 **가상 캐릭터 "무전술 대감독"** 으로 압축한다.

## Satire Character

### 무전술 대감독

Fictional composite character. He represents public criticism around vague tactics, passive in-game management, late adjustment, and evasive press-conference language.

Profile:

- Visual: generic middle-aged football manager silhouette in a plain dark tracksuit.
- Face: non-specific, no real likeness.
- Props: blank tactic board, unused whistle, stopwatch that never starts, folded substitution card.
- Gesture: points confidently at the blank board, then hides behind it.
- Attitude: calm, ceremonial, evasive, oddly proud of doing nothing.
- Core joke: every answer sounds authoritative, but nothing concrete happens.

Catchphrase direction:

```text
전술은 간단하다. 정신력으로 간다.
```

Do not use:

- Real coach name.
- Real face, hairstyle, body shape, press photo composition, or recognizable outfit.
- Personal insults about appearance, age, family, health, or private life.
- Claims of corruption, bribery, intentional sabotage, or criminal behavior.

### 조선 선비 전술관

The scholar is the audience lens. He makes vague football language literal.

- "경우의 수" -> opens a civil-service-exam scroll.
- "정신력" -> brings a battlefield drum.
- "늦은 교체" -> checks a sundial.
- "VAR" -> bows as if receiving a royal edict.
- "전술 보완" -> tries to fill the blank board with brush strokes.
- "붉은 응원단" -> gets overexcited in the stands, then enters a fantasy version
  of the pitch and scores the goal the tactic board never planned.

## Marketing Rationale

- Timely: Korea's exit and coaching criticism are current conversation.
- Comment-friendly: viewers can argue "감독 문제 vs 협회 문제 vs 선수 문제".
- Visually readable: locker room, tactic board, scroll, sundial, VAR screen, press table.
- Safer satire: targets systems and public discourse, not a real person's face.

## Tone Guardrails

Allowed:

- "전술판은 비어 있었다"
- "교체 타이밍은 해시계가 정했다"
- "정신력이 전술보다 먼저 투입됐다"
- "경우의 수만 조선 팔도만큼 길었다"
- "응원하다가 상상 속 경기장으로 뛰쳐나갔다"

Avoid:

- Direct real-name mockery.
- "무능", "바보", "사기", "비리" as direct assertions.
- Real team logos, real sponsors, real player faces.
- Violence against the coach or players.
- Depicting a realistic illegal pitch invasion as admirable or actionable. If the
  scholar enters the pitch, make it clearly fantasy, dream, replay, or tactical-board
  imagination.
