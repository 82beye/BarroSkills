## EP03 Draft: PC방 간 조선 선비

Working title:

- `EP03 - 조선 선비 PC방 입성`
- `JOSEON SCHOLAR ENTERS PC BANG`

Scene beats:

1. Scholar opens PC bang door and freezes under RGB lighting.
2. He tries to press the keyboard with a calligraphy brush.
3. He wears a headset and commands teammates like a battlefield general.
4. He discovers ramen and canned drinks, mistaking the PC bang for a tavern.
5. He loses a match and overreacts, hat and sleeves shaking.
6. CTA: "다음엔 어디 보낼까요?"

Required framing:

- Vertical 9:16.
- Wide-angle 24mm look.
- Full body visible when action matters.
- Show PC monitors, keyboard, headset, ramen, and RGB environment.
